Here is a sample content of a letter for an application of a sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a sick leave for [number] days, starting from [start date] to [end date], as I am currently unwell and unable to come to work.

Due to [briefly mention the reason for your illness, e.g. fever, flu, doctor's appointment, etc.], I am not feeling well enough to perform my duties at the office. I am undergoing [mention any medical treatment or consultation, if applicable] and my doctor has advised me to take complete rest for a few days.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will keep you updated on my status and will provide a doctor's note, if required.

I would like to request your approval for my sick leave and appreciate your understanding in this matter.

Thank you for your support and cooperation.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that this is a sample content and you should modify it as per your needs and circumstances. Also, make sure to keep a professional tone and format throughout the letter.
